FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Will the systems make me a winner?

No guarantees. The systems are tools whose full backtest and forward records are public; results depend on market conditions and your own decisions. That is exactly why OKUGAI keeps publishing both the good results and the bad in the resident ledger. You run them in your own account, at your own judgment.

Do you send trade signals (buy/sell instructions)?

No "buy here, sell here" instructions or recommendations. Residents broadcast their own trades live in the booth, but this is content for observation and learning — not a recommendation to copy the trades. All trading decisions are yours (individual trade consultations are also out of scope).

Is it OK for beginners?

Yes. Newcomer's Alley has a beginner roadmap covering account mechanics, order types and money management from the ground up. Frankly, we'd rather you meet the "verify before you use it" culture before bad habits set in.

Can I keep up with a full-time job?

Yes. Everything is archived and a weekly digest lands in the Command Room, so you can catch up on weekends. Nothing requires real-time attendance (we don't broadcast trade calls, so there is nothing to sit glued to by design).

Will I definitely become profitable?

No — and we answer that honestly. FX carries the risk of losing your capital, and nobody can guarantee results. Beware of anyone who promises them. What the hideout provides is the skill and environment to verify and judge a strategy's expected value yourself.

Can I leave (cancel) anytime?

You can stop renewal anytime from the payment platform (Stripe) billing page. Billing is monthly with no lock-in: stop renewal and your residency ends at the close of the current month (no pro-rata refunds), with full access until the last day you've paid for. You can always return — though OKU-SO, being capacity-limited, requires a fresh review to re-enter.
